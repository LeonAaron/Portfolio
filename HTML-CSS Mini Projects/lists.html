Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a great recipie.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter melted, add cream and eggs
  4. Use spatula to fold eggs and fold to center every 20 seconds
  5. When eggs still moist remove them from heat
